"Colorful conservation for your backyard and beyond!"

SeedPodz are seeds packed in nutrient rich, handmade podz that break apart in the rain.

  • 🌱 No colors or harmful additives
  • 🌱 Safe for wildlife and pets
  • 🌱 Uniquely shaped to stay where they land; won't roll downhill
  • 🌱 Proven germination rates - Seeds remain viable for at least 2 years
