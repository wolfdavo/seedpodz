Simple ingredients with profound impact! Compost, clay and seeds. The compost and clay act as a carrier for the seeds. We’ve chosen a compost that holds essential starting nutrients for the unique blend of seeds. Likewise, we’ve chosen a clay that won’t bring strong elements to the mix and burn, damage or kill the seeds. The clay is the binder that holds everything together until the magical touch of the rain. If there’s too much clay the Seedpodz will need a terential rain to break it open and the footprint of growth is smaller. So we’ve created the perfect mix with the perfect ingredients for your success!
